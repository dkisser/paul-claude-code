## 9. 卡片式 (Card-based)

Card-based layout with subtle shadows, rounded corners, clear hierarchy. White cards on light gray backgrounds. Consistent 8-16px rounded corners. Subtle drop shadows (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)). Clear card boundaries with 16-24px internal padding. Grid-based card arrangement with consistent gaps. Cards as content containers with distinct actions. Hover states with elevated shadows. Consistent card sizing or masonry layout. Borders as secondary separators. Focus on scannable, modular content blocks.
